About The Game

A 60 minute, live game for 3-10 people. Go on a quest through time and space, solving puzzles, challenges and mysteries.

Along your game, you will encounter puzzles, challenges and mysteries – fiendish obstacles designed to test your ingenuity and teamwork. But, don’t forget, you’re against the clock. Complete your mission and get out – before time runs out.

The goal is simple yet challenging: get out in time! Else ... you could be trapped inside forever! During this engaging and challenging game team members truly live and breathe in union for an hour.
